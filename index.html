While marijuana is not
officially legal in

13,000
registered users to

cannabis. So

only pharmacy
licensed to distribute
medical
marijuana

at Eilat's Yoseftal
Medical Center. But

Ministry of
Health is hoping

medical
marijuana
through
pharmacies across Israel
sometime in
2013.
